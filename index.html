Walking the Wainwrights

Located in the English national park ‘ The Lake District’, the Wainwright fells continue to delight walkers with their varied landscapes, charm, peace and exhilaration just as they did when Alfred Wainwright wrote his guide books.

Learn more about Alfred Wainwright and the Wainwright fells. Find inspiration for your next/first walk and tips for looking after the fells and keeping yourself safe.
